# Introduction

Administrators are expected to maintain the shared AWS infrastructure that makes the lab's research workflows safe, reproducible, and cost-aware. Administrative access is highly privileged and should be granted only to people who understand these standards.

To request administrative access, contact an [Owner](../index.md#aws-owners) or [Administrator](../index.md#aws-administrators). Routine research usage is documented in the [Researchers section](../researchers/introduction.md).
